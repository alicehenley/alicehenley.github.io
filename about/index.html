Who am I?   

Hi there!

I'm an outdoorsy PhD student currently in Nottingham, UK writing up my thesis while procrastinating by writing about my escapades on here!

I work in physical chemistry measuring how biologically-relevant molecules interact with light using laser spectroscopy in the lab and employ computational simulations with quantum chemistry approaches to aid in the interpretation of the results. Head over to my research page to read more about that. I recently had my tenth publication accepted and I'm quite proud of it!

All the while, I try and get outside in my hiking boots or on my bike as much as possible. I love camping and being away from all the hustle and bustle! Head over to my adventures page to read what I've been up to.

This website serves as a way for me to improve my writing, showcase some pictures and maybe even help someone in the future. I look forward to updating with posts on what I've up to and I hope you enjoy reading.
